About Shiny Side Up

A cleaning partner grounded in care, consistency, and community

Shiny Side Up is a Wodonga-born team that treats every home, Airbnb, and workplace like our own. We combine high standards, reliable scheduling, and respectful service so you can relax knowing the details are handled.

Our promise

We know that letting someone into your space requires trust. Our cleaners are local, fully insured, and trained to follow detailed checklists so nothing is left to chance. From first booking to final inspection, we keep communication clear and timelines tight.

What to expect

  • ›Upfront pricing with no surprise add-ons.
  • ›On-time arrivals and proactive schedule reminders.
  • ›Eco-friendly products on request and safe handling for pets and kids.
  • ›Photo-ready finishes for Airbnb turnovers and vacate cleans.
  • ›A friendly crew who respects your home and privacy.

Values that guide every clean

Consistency

We use repeatable checklists and supervisor spot-checks to deliver the same spotless result every visit.

Respect

From removing shoes to locking up securely, we treat your space, neighbours, and belongings with care.

Community

We’re proud locals who sponsor Border events and source supplies from nearby businesses where possible.

Covering the Albury-Wodonga region

From family homes to short-stay apartments and commercial sites, our crews service Wodonga, Albury, Thurgoona, Lavington, Baranduda, and nearby townships. Need emergency turnover support? We keep a rapid-response team on rotation for urgent bookings.
