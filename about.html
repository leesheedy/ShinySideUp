Our Story

Founded in 2017 by Border locals Jess and Aaron, Shiny Side Up began with a simple mission: to deliver hotel-standard cleaning with a neighbourly touch.

2017 — Humble Beginnings

Started with one car, a vacuum, and a passion for sparkling spaces across Wodonga and Albury.

2019 — Growing Team

Expanded to a dedicated team of five, introduced eco-certified products, and partnered with local NDIS providers.

2022 — Commercial Expansion

Added commercial and construction cleaning services, servicing offices, schools, and hospitality venues.

Today

More than 20 trained cleaners servicing 300+ properties each month, still proudly family-owned and community focused.
